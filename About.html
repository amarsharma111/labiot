IOT-CLUB
INNOVATOR'S EVOLUTION

  • IoT Club serves as a public place where students can discuss, learn about, and work on IoT related projects.
  • IoT club members are offered a series of programs and activities such as seminars, workshops, short courses, certificates, hackathons, project competitions and disclosures.
  • The club is supported by both internal and external professionals who help students plan strategies and activities that will help them with knowledge and skills in IoT.
  • The club aim to guide students and prepare them to pursue their professional careers in the professional industry.
  • Internet of things is a part of Industry 4.0 technologies and it is creating an environment of convergence in society.
  • IoT Club is an initiative to bring this technology closer to students. IoT club aims to encourage students and train them in IoT and related domains.
  • IoT club organizes the events like seminars, workshops, hands-on trainings, competitions and many more.
  • Being a part of the IoT club, helps you to master the IoT concepts and being a part of IoT enthusiasts.
